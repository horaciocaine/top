Setting Cc: Recipients

Cc: recipients receive a copy of the message and are set with the setCc() method of the message.

To set Cc: recipients:

Call the setCc() method on the Message.

Cc: recipients are visible in the message headers and will be seen by the other recipients. 

$message->setCc(array(
  'person1@example.org',
  'person2@otherdomain.org' => 'Person 2 Name',
  'person3@example.org',
  'person4@example.org',
  'person5@example.org' => 'Person 5 Name'
));
